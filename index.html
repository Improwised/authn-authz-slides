OIDC

Open ID Connect verifies that users are who they say they are

OIDC is an identity authentication protocol that is an extension of open authorization (OAuth) 2.0

OAuth 2.0

OAuth stands for Open Authorization

OAuth is a technological standard that allows you to authorize one app or service to sign in to another without divulging private information, such as passwords.

RBAC

Role-based access control (RBAC) refers to the idea of assigning permissions to users based on their role within an organization. It offers a simple, manageable approach to access management that is less prone to error than assigning permissions to users individually.
